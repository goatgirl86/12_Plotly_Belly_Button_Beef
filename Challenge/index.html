Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard

A Peak at the the Possibilities of Improbable Beef

What's This All About?

Rosa is looking to discover and document species of bacteria in the belly button that can replicate the taste of beef. If successful, synthetic "belly button beef" may be the new diet fad.This dashboard is only the initial step in Rosa's efforts to discover the ultimate Improbable Beef substitute. More more research is needed to complete her mission.
